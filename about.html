About Us

Who We Are?

JAWAHER METALS FACTORY (JMF) is a leading company in the metal industries, specializing in stainless steel, iron, copper, and other architectural crafts and decorations. Using the latest technologies such as cutting, welding, casting, molding, turnings, carvings, and electroplating, we are able to provide contemporary designs with high-quality finishing and installation.


Established in 1985 by businessman Mohammed M. Bakro, JMF started as a specialized workshop providing metal plating services for jewelry and accessories. In just a few years, JMF expanded its capabilities to include industrial plating, becoming one of the outstanding and unique companies in the metals field in the region. In 1997, JMF established a new factory in the Baghlaf industrial area in Al Khobar, adding metal design and fabrication to its portfolio.

How We Work?

Today, JMF is considered one of the leading firms in the metals works industry, specializing in stainless steel, brass, and aluminum architecture works. Our unique advantage lies in our extensive experience in all kinds of metals electroplating, which we have built through study, years of work, and the implementation of distinctive projects.


Our team of dedicated professionals includes specialists in mechanical polishing for metals, architectural modeling from various metals, and a specialized department for computer-aided design and manufacturing (CAD/CAM) that includes laser, plasma, water jet, and router machines. We can cut, drill, and engrave different thicknesses and sizes of metals, and provide professional services for inscriptions, cavernous reliefs, and other decorations. In addition, we own special equipment for casting and turning that can meet the needs of both light and heavy industries.

Our Commitments

At JAWAHER, we are committed to delivering high-quality products that meet the needs of our customers with the highest international standards and specifications. Our team has extensive and distinguished experience in manufacturing and implementation, supported by architects and specialists who manage and follow up on all stages of projects until finalizing all details.


Furthermore, Mohammed M. Bakro's son, Odai Bakro, is also involved in running the company, and is dedicated to keeping his father's legacy alive by continuously improving the factory and its services. With thirty years of formulating and evolving specialist team skills, JAWAHER continues to provide exceptional services in the metals industry.
